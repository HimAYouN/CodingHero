About Mood Board

Mood Board is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you track and reflect on your daily moods. By logging your feelings every day, you can easily visualize your emotional journey over the course of the month. At the end of each month, you’ll have a clear view of your mood patterns, which can inspire positive changes and help you stay motivated to focus on your mental well-being.

This app was built with Angular, marking an exciting step in my learning journey. Throughout the development process, I’ve gained valuable experience with Angular’s powerful features and tools, and I’m thrilled to share this project as part of my growth as a developer.
